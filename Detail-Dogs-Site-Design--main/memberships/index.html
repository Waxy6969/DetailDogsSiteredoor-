What Is A Maintenance Detail?

For vehicles already kept in good condition.

Maintenance services protect and refresh vehicles that are already being cared for. If a vehicle requires restoration-level work, an upgrade service may be recommended.

  • Does not include heavy pet hair removal
  • Does not include excessive trash removal
  • Does not include stain extraction
  • Does not include mold or biohazard cleanup
  • Does not include severe salt or mud buildup

Why Maintenance Matters

Consistency beats occasional deep cleans.

Most vehicle damage happens from neglect. Dirt scratches paint, UV dries interiors, salt causes corrosion, and oils degrade surfaces. Regular maintenance prevents expensive corrections later.
